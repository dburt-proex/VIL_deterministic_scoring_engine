Core Principle

The Scoring Invariant

Every signal is evaluated against two dimensions: operational value and evidence quality. The final score is always constrained by verifiability.

vil_score = min(weighted_signal_score, verifiability_score)

A signal cannot outrank its evidence.

Signal Routing

Five Deterministic Routes

Every evaluated signal exits VIL through exactly one route. No ambiguity, no guesswork.

PASS
High-confidence signal. Ready for downstream action.
8.0 – 10.0
REVIEW
Valuable or plausible signal requiring human judgment.
5.0 – 7.99
CLARIFY
Potentially useful, but missing evidence or specificity.
3.0 – 4.99
ARCHIVE
Low-value noise. Store or discard.
0.0 – 2.99
HALT
Unsafe, non-compliant, destructive, or outside scope.
Critical risk flag

  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "signal_type": "lead_intake",
    "source": "website_form",
    "content": "Enterprise client requesting AI workflow audit",
    "metadata": {
      "budget_mentioned": true,
      "company_size": "500+",
      "timeline": "Q3 2025"
    }
  }'

Applications

Where VIL Operates

VIL eliminates cognitive noise at the intake layer. One expensive queue at a time.

Lead Qualification

Score and route inbound leads before they consume operator attention. Separate high-intent signals from noise.

Construction RFI Triage

Evaluate request-for-information items by urgency, verifiability, and risk before assigning to project managers.

Support Prioritization

Rank tickets by evidence quality and operational impact. Route critical issues immediately, archive noise.

Research Intake

Assess research signals for strategic alignment and evidence before committing resources to investigation.

Workflow Pre-Checking

Validate that workflow triggers carry enough context and confidence before allowing downstream automation.

Content Opportunity Scoring

Evaluate content ideas against strategic criteria and revenue potential before production investment.

Architecture

Built for Precision

Lightweight, deterministic, auditable. No ML black boxes. No probabilistic guesswork.

FastAPI Service

Production-ready REST API with health checks, structured error handling, and JSON schema validation.

Deterministic Engine

Pure scoring logic. No model inference, no hallucination, no drift. Same input always produces same output.

Configurable Weights

YAML-driven criteria weights and threshold configuration. Tune per deployment without code changes.

Audit Trail

Every routing decision generates a complete audit record: scores, criteria breakdowns, risk flags, timestamps.

Risk Overrides

Critical flags override scoring entirely. Dangerous signals route to HALT regardless of value score.

Docker Ready

Single-command deployment. Container-native architecture for production, staging, and CI environments.
